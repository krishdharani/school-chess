How to Play

  1. One player clicks "Create New Game" to start a new game
  2. Share the Game ID with your friend
  3. Your friend enters the Game ID and clicks "Join Game"
  4. The creator plays as White, the joiner plays as Black
  5. Take turns moving pieces by dragging them
  6. Checkmate ends the game
